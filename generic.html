Radio stations
from around the world

As a radio enthusiast, I’ve always searched for stations that play the music I love. That’s why I decided to create this section of the site — to share the radio stations from around the world that I personally connect with. These are stations that broadcast rock music from the 80s and 90s, with minimal or no commercials.
.

https://www.rockfm.fm/

ROCK FM is a Spanish radio station that broadcasts classic and modern rock music. Known for its focus on rock hits from the 80s, 90s, and beyond, the station offers high-energy pl aylists with minimal interruptions.

https://www.atticaradios.gr/rockfm969/

A Spanish radio station broadcasting on 96.9 FM, known for its rock-focused playlists featuring hits from the 80s, 90s, and today. Offers a high-energy rock experience with minimal commercial breaks.

https://www.radio886.at/

A dynamic rock radio station broadcasting from Austria, offering channels such as Hard Rock, Classic Rock, New Rock & Metal through its web‑streams.

https://q945allrock.com/

Q94.5 All Rock (KFRQ FM) broadcasts classic and modern rock music — mainly hits from bands like AC/DC, Guns N’ Roses, Metallica, Nirvana, and Foo Fighters. It’s a Texas-based radio station known for playing “all rock, all the time”, focusing on popular rock from the ’70s to today.
